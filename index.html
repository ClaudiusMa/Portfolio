My Story

Growing up in a family of a veteran and a professor of public administration, I was instilled with the principle of contributing to the benefit of society. As an interdisciplinary designer, I made products that helped combat the prejudice Chinese Designers can experience, redefining design, and finally, helping people make better choices when inundated with information overload.
