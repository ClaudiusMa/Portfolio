My Story

I am obsessed with experimentation. My friends say I am always coming up with hypotheses-even about our daily lives and relationships. This serves me well as a product person, because I always come up with a question and find the solution through experimentation.


The products I made are generally incubated in the process of testing hypotheses, and the answers to my question are presented by the work I made during the process of inquiry. In the past five years I have asked two questions, made three hypotheses, and have made real impacts on my community.
