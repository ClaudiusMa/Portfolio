. 1
Elegance

Elegance is one of my works in The Heritage Series of Chow Tai Fook. The Heritage Series is mainly based on the totem pattern in the Tang Dynasty in China, and uses the symbolic language to convey subjective feelings.
Elegance originated from The Classic of Poetry, also Shijing. The craft in the middle is called Wu, which embodies reputation. You can see four big characters in the middle of Wu. They are small seal scripts. Our ancestors used this proverb to describe elegant women. The Wu is surrounded by Lotus and Phoenix, which are the symbols of nobleness and purity. I combine the symbolic language as a panegyric to women.

. 2
Twirling

Twirling is also one of my works in The Heritage Series of Chow Tai Fook. Inspiration originated from the parrots and flowers in the Tang Dynasty. In the context of ancient China, parrots often come together in pairs, and the meaning of twirling is to wish the couple's love forever. Telling stories with symbols of arts and crafts is a form of Chinese design, and I like how crafts can share a story without words to people.

. 3
Process

The design process started from the research of a certain dynasty, and then brainstorming a lot of ideas. The ideation process is similar to writing poetry. The only difference is we express the ideas or feelings through an artistic and symbolic language. Next, we must cooperate with the marketing department to make the story simpler and more attractive, so that even people who have not heard the story can understand it. Then, we worked with the production team to ensure the technical feasibility of the art works. Finally, it was produced and made for sale.
