TCL Smart TV

Context

In the process of exploration, I gradually discovered that the symbols of ancient culture were based on feudal monarchy, and their design objects were often aimed at the royal family. Ancient Chinese designs should not be used to form the symbolic style of modern China. Modern China must discover its own design for its own era. As the design should represent the current challenges that the modern world faces, I start to further discover modern design.

Project Brief

In 2019, IoT is a term that has been pushed to the forefront of technology. Smart homes are a new field for everyone. Its design framework is vague and there is more room to expand and explore. While designing at TCL Industrial design center, my team and I were aiming to make product upgrades for C series Smart TVs. Our design goal was to make Smart TVs more valued.

Design

Design Description

As a container-like object, TV does not directly interact with people. People interact with the media produced for TV. Therefore, the expression of "value" may focus on the interaction between two objects, between objects and their environment. We used the Lotus Blossom Ideation Process and discovered some keywords of ‚Äúvalue‚Äù and I mainly focused on three of them.

1. Soft: make the TV softer through the use of fabrics and wooden materials.
2. Airy: Make the TV look less heavy through the use of acrylic materials in the base.
3. Color: Choose neutral colors and colors suitable for the environment, so the TV blends into the environment.
